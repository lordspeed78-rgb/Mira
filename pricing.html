Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Mira for free?

Absolutely! The Free plan is free forever and gives you access to our basic AR tools, perfect for getting started and exploring the platform.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as UPI, Net Banking, and PayPal for seamless transactions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle, and you won't be charged again.

Do I get updates with Pro and Business plans?

Yes! All paying customers receive immediate access to all platform updates. Pro and Business users also get early access to beta features before they're released to everyone else.

Is there a student or non-profit discount?

We believe in supporting education and social causes. Please contact our support team with your credentials, and we'll be happy to discuss available discounts.
